About Us

FlippingBooks is a platform for students to buy and sell texts. The intent of FlippingBooks is to provide a free, fast, and effective way for students to save and recoup money by buying used textbooks while also being able to sell their old unwanted books. In essence, this site is made to flip books.

This project was inspired by the lack of user friendly way of selling and buying textbooks! We hope you enjoy using this site as much as we've enjoyed creating it for you!
