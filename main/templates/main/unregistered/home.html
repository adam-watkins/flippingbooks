Welcome to Flipping Books!

FlippingBooks is a platform for students to buy and sell texts. The intent of FlippingBooks is to provide a free, fast, and effective way for students to save and recoup money by buying used textsbooks while also being able to sell their old unwanted books. In essence, this site is made to flip books.

You must login to view the seller's contact information.
